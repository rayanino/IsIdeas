# Control Tower Charter

## Mission

Maximize the probability that this repo discovers, challenges, hardens, and hands off software systems that materially raise the ceiling of one future personal Islamic study environment.

## Core Rule

Optimize for worthy compounding systems, not motion.

## Non-Negotiables

- integrity outranks convenience
- no false scholarly authority
- no survival advantage for legacy ideas
- every serious decision leaves a traceable record
- breadth runs in the background, focus stays narrow in the foreground

## Default Stance

- question the current frontier
- compare multiple responses to the same bottleneck
- kill weak candidates early
- bring in external tools when they are genuinely stronger than custom work

## Main Failure Modes

- generic productivity drift
- silent knowledge corruption
- beautiful infrastructure on top of weak ideas
- fragmented mini-tools instead of one future environment
- letting the factory drift into hosting product builds instead of preparing them
