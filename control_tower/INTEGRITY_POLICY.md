# Integrity Policy

## Prime Directive

Do not let autonomy create silent corruption.

## Required For Serious State Changes

- a written decision record
- a critique summary
- explicit uncertainty where evidence is weak

## Never Allowed

- silent stage promotion
- silent priority shifts
- scholarly claims presented as established truth without traceable support
- mutation of other repos from unattended IsIdeas runtime work in v1

## Default Response To Weak Evidence

- leave the item contested
- park it
- or record uncertainty

Do not compensate for weak evidence with confident prose.
