# Operating Cadences

These are the documented practices the operator follows to keep the factory honest and moving. They are not running software.

## Cadences

### Intake Triage
- **Rhythm**: twice daily
- **Purpose**: Process new owner submissions, assign initial stage, route to workspace or reject.
- **Failure looks like**: submissions pile up unprocessed; ideas enter the registry without triage.

### Bottleneck Map Refresh
- **Rhythm**: daily
- **Purpose**: Re-examine whether the current bottleneck framing still holds and whether leverage scores are honest.
- **Failure looks like**: the bottleneck map goes stale; frontier ideas drift without grounding.

### Tool Scouting
- **Rhythm**: daily
- **Purpose**: Survey external tools and research that could replace custom work or unblock a dependency.
- **Failure looks like**: the factory reinvents what already exists; tool evaluations go stale.

### Red-Team Critique
- **Rhythm**: daily
- **Purpose**: Challenge the current frontier idea with independent adversarial pressure.
- **Failure looks like**: frontier ideas self-confirm; no critique artifact is attached to promotions.

### Morning Report Generation
- **Rhythm**: daily
- **Purpose**: Produce a concise summary of factory state, integrity flags, and next moves.
- **Failure looks like**: sessions start without context; integrity issues go unnoticed.

### Dossier Hardening
- **Rhythm**: daily
- **Purpose**: Strengthen the strongest surviving dossiers with deeper evidence, sharper boundaries, and explicit open questions.
- **Failure looks like**: dossiers stagnate at surface-level analysis; specs never become implementation-grade.

### Spec Readiness Checks
- **Rhythm**: daily
- **Purpose**: Evaluate whether dossier-stage ideas meet the criteria for spec-ready promotion.
- **Failure looks like**: ideas linger at dossier stage without clear promotion or rejection signals.

### Frontier Degradation Alerts
- **Rhythm**: daily
- **Purpose**: Detect when the current frontier idea is weakening under new evidence or critique.
- **Failure looks like**: a weak frontier idea consumes attention long after it should have been parked or replaced.

### Stale Research Detection
- **Rhythm**: daily
- **Purpose**: Identify research artifacts, tool evaluations, or open questions that have gone unreviewed too long.
- **Failure looks like**: decisions rest on outdated evidence; the research surface quietly rots.

## Principle

Breadth cadences should keep discovering and comparing.
Foreground cadences should stay narrow enough that the frontier can actually be judged.
