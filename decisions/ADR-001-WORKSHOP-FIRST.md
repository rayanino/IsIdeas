# ADR-001 — Workshop-first repository architecture

## Status
Accepted

## Decision
The repository will be structured primarily as a high-rigor idea workshop rather than as a flat collection of final specs.

## Why
The main strategic risk is promoting weak ideas too early and wasting the three-year preparation window.
A workshop-first design better supports:
- incubation,
- pressure-testing,
- pruning,
- and delayed promotion.

## Consequence
The repo emphasizes lifecycle, dossiers, quality gates, and handoff discipline before implementation-oriented artifacts.
