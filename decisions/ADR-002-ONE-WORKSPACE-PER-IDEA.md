# ADR-002 — One workspace per idea

## Status
Accepted

## Decision
Each meaningful idea should get its own directory under `ideas/<idea-slug>/`.

## Why
A dedicated workspace gives an idea room for:
- status tracking,
- dossier development,
- optional research notes,
- optional session history,
- and eventual specification.

This prevents the repository from collapsing into flat, context-poor idea files.

## Consequence
Ideas can mature at different rates without being forced into the same artifact shape too early.
