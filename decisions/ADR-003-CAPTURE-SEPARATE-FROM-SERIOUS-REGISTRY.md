# ADR-003 — Capture is separate from the serious registry

## Status
Accepted

## Decision
Raw idea intake will live under `capture/` and will not enter `catalog/IDEA_REGISTRY.md` until it survives an initial judgment.

## Why
The serious registry should remain a curated view of ideas worth real architectural attention.
Mixing raw sparks with serious ideas would blur status and weaken prioritization.

## Consequence
The repo now distinguishes preservation from promotion more clearly.
