# ADR-004 — Focus is intentionally narrow

## Status
Accepted

## Decision
The repository will maintain one primary deep-focus idea and a narrow band of actively attended ideas.

## Why
The scarce resource is disciplined attention, not storage. Narrow focus improves dossier quality, review quality, and prioritization honesty.

## Consequence
`catalog/ACTIVE_FOCUS.md`, the priority board, and focus-discipline rules become operational parts of the workshop rather than decorative artifacts.
