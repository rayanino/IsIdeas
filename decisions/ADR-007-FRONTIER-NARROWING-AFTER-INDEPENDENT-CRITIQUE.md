# ADR-007 — Narrow the frontier after dual independent critique

## Status
Accepted

## Decision
After running independent critique on both frontier ideas, narrow deep attention back to `Fixed-Text Preservation System` alone.
Demote `Curriculum Architect` from `frontier/deep` to `challenge/visible`.

## Why

The new critique loop produced materially different outcomes:

- `Fixed-Text Preservation System`: mixed, but still plausibly strong if hardened
- `Curriculum Architect`: weakened more substantially by unvalidated modelability and authority assumptions

The correct response is not to kill Curriculum Architect, but to remove scarce deep attention from it until those assumptions are tested directly.

## Consequence

- active deep attention becomes singular again
- the next hardening lane is the fixed-text dossier
- Curriculum Architect remains preserved, but must re-earn frontier status through concrete validation rather than narrative strength
