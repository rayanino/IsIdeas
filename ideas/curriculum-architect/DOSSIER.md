# Curriculum Architect — Dossier

## Working Thesis
Build a system that helps a student move through an Islamic science in the right order, with explicit prerequisite awareness and stage discipline.

## Why This Looks Strong
A huge amount of wasted study effort comes from bad sequencing rather than lack of effort.

## What Must Be Clarified
- what exactly counts as a prerequisite,
- how multiple valid paths should be represented,
- where teacher guidance should override system logic,
- what the minimal MVP really is.

## Main Risk
This idea could become falsely authoritative if it presents one path as the path.

## Current Judgment
Very promising, but not mature enough for implementation handoff.
