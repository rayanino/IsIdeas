# Memorization OS — Dossier

## Working Thesis
Build a memorization management system tailored to Islamic texts that helps preserve memorized material over years through better segmentation, review timing, and weak-point visibility.

## Why This Looks Strong
Memorization remains central in many serious study paths, and poor review discipline quietly destroys previous gains.

## What Must Be Clarified
- what the right memorization unit is,
- how review scheduling should work,
- how to avoid fake precision in retention scoring,
- what should be teacher-adjustable.

## Main Risk
The system could optimize for app activity rather than actual memorization quality.

## Current Judgment
High-potential idea, but the internal logic still needs much more precision before spec work.
