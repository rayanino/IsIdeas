# Question & Confusion Ledger — Dossier

## Working Thesis
Build a system that captures, classifies, and preserves points of confusion so they can be resolved deliberately instead of being forgotten or blurred.

## Why This Looks Strong
A large part of serious learning is noticing exactly what is not yet understood and dealing with it carefully.

## What Must Be Clarified
- what confusion types are actually useful,
- what counts as tentative versus real resolution,
- how source linking should work,
- how to prioritize unresolved items.

## Main Risk
The system could create the illusion of resolution while merely storing notes.

## Current Judgment
Strong candidate, but still needs sharper logic and boundaries before it deserves specification work.
