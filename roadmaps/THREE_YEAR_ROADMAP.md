# Three-Year Roadmap

This roadmap assumes the main study-preparation phase happens during the computer science degree, with serious Islamic study becoming central after graduation.

## Year 1 — Build the workshop properly
Focus on repository architecture, shared primitives, quality discipline, and the first dossiers.

## Year 2 — Convert the best dossiers into real specs
Focus on maturing the strongest ideas into implementation-grade handoff packets.

## Year 3 — Build and integrate selectively
Focus on only the highest-leverage systems and make them work together coherently.

## Standing Rule
The success metric is not number of repos or features.
The success metric is entering the study phase with unusually strong intellectual infrastructure.
