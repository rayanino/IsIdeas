# Audit Checklist

Use this checklist during portfolio or architecture reviews.

## Idea-Level Audit
- does each serious idea have a clear current stage?
- does each serious idea have a real next move?
- does the registry reflect actual state?
- is any dossier drifting or stale?
- is any idea consuming attention without real leverage?

## Portfolio-Level Audit
- is the current primary focus still justified?
- are there too many active fronts?
- are priority bands still correct?
- is a blocked idea occupying too much mental space?
- is a parked or rejected idea worth revisiting?

## Architecture-Level Audit
- are the repo rules still helping rather than cluttering?
- are stage semantics still being used correctly?
- are naming conventions holding?
- is the `kr` boundary still clear?
- are decisions being preserved when they matter?

## Hygiene Rule
An audit should end with explicit consequences, not just observations.
