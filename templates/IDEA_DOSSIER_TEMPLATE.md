# Idea Dossier Template

Use this template once an idea deserves a serious living working document.

## Working Thesis
What is the current best one-paragraph statement of the idea?

## Why This Might Matter
Why might this create real long-term leverage?

## The Bottleneck
What exact recurring problem does this seem to attack?

## Why Software Might Be The Right Tool
What can software do here that ordinary notes, memory, or discipline alone cannot do as well?

## Why This Might Be Strong
List the strongest arguments in favor.

## Why This Might Be Weak
List the strongest objections.

## Boundary Questions
What exactly belongs inside this system, and what should stay outside it?

## Data / Entity Questions
What entities, objects, or relationships seem central?

## Main Risks
How could this mislead, overreach, distract, or create false confidence?

## Relationship To `kr`
How does this depend on, extend, or stay distinct from `kr`?

## Competing / Alternative Approaches
What other ways might the same bottleneck be attacked?

## Current Open Questions
What still needs deep thinking or research?
