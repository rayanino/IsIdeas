# Stage Definition of Done

This document defines the minimum condition for truthfully calling an idea by a given stage.

These are not perfection standards.
They are honesty standards.

## Spark — Definition of Done
An idea may be called a Spark only if:
- it has been captured clearly enough to be understood later,
- it has at least a rough statement of the bottleneck it might attack,
- and it is still explicitly provisional.

## Incubating — Definition of Done
An idea may be called Incubating only if:
- it has a dedicated workspace,
- it has a named bottleneck,
- it has a plausible leverage case,
- it has a current next move,
- and it is worth real discussion rather than mere preservation.

## Dossier — Definition of Done
An idea may be called Dossier-stage only if:
- it has a living `DOSSIER.md`,
- the strongest arguments for and against are being recorded honestly,
- main risks are named,
- key open questions are visible,
- relation to `kr` is at least being considered,
- and the dossier is actively helping judgment rather than merely storing notes.

## Spec-ready — Definition of Done
An idea may be called Spec-ready only if:
- the problem is precise,
- the leverage case remains strong after challenge,
- scope boundaries are visible,
- the data model is becoming intelligible,
- the major failure modes are named,
- the MVP shape is plausible,
- and there is a deliberate decision that formal specification work should now begin.

A completed `SPEC.md` is not yet required for Spec-ready.
That belongs to the next stage.

## Handoff-ready — Definition of Done
An idea may be called Handoff-ready only if:
- it has a mature `SPEC.md`,
- correctness constraints are explicit,
- dependencies are known,
- success criteria are testable,
- and Claude Code would not need to guess about the fundamentals of what is being built.

## Parked — Definition of Done
An idea may be called Parked only if:
- it is no longer live in the serious registry,
- the reason for parking is recorded,
- and the condition for future revisiting is recorded if one exists.

## Rejected — Definition of Done
An idea may be called Rejected only if:
- it is no longer live in the serious registry,
- the reason for rejection is recorded,
- and the rejection is deliberate rather than accidental neglect.
